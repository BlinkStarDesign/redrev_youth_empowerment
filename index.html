About Us


The RedRev Youth Empowerment Corporation was formed in 2018 by Children of War Veterans in Zimbabwe with the support of the Government. Our goal is to play a central role as the Youth in developing our great Nation into a middle-income Economy by the year 2030. Our investments vehicle currently has active commercial projects in the Sustainability (Green Economy), Mining and beneficiation, Agri-Business, ICT, Energy and Tourism sectors. We now have an Offshore Office in Chelmsford, UK, and active engagements are being made to engage with International Capital Markets to bring back investment in line with the Zimbabwe Open for Business Policy.

Agri-Business

Under this Business Unit, we focus on the Production, Processing, Packaging, and Distribution of agricultural produce to local, regional and international markets.


FARMER SYNDICATES

RedRev Youth Empowerment Corp is currently working with the Ministry of Agriculture under MOU towards the facilitation of the AGRI-HUBS Programme.


- Hampton Farm HUB currently undergoing an infrastructure overhaul.
- US$ 80K earmarked for production & infrastructure development of AGRI-HUB Facilities in 2024.

Belarus Tractors Mechanisation Programme

- Zimbabwe received at least 2 000 High-tech tractros, 80 combine harvesters and other agricultural equipment from Belarus over the past 3 years.
- The equipment, which is now available in all the country’s districts, has been supplied at concessional rates under Phases 1 and 2 of the Belarus Farm Mechanisation Facility.
- Under Phase 3, whose implementation has commenced, 4 000 more tractors are expected to be delivered, starting this year.
- REDREV YOUTH through it's Partnership with the YOUTH EMPOWERBANK will be offering eligible Members and Farmers 81HP TRACTORS under the ongoing Belarus Mechanisation Scheme.

CRS PROGRAMMES

Alcohol Level & Substance Abuse Initiative (ALSA)

• This programme aims to actively address and reduce the impact of an epidemic that hass sweeped a whole nation. Drugs, alcohol and substances has managed to become a burden on a social, economic, healyh and security level of nation.
• Data Compilation: RedRev Youth will be laying the ground for the ALSA initiatve through the compilation of Data Surveys
• Raising Awareness: RedRev will be using the following methods in its Awareness Campaigns: Social Media, Hosting of Fundraisers & Social Events, Engaging in Corporate Partnerships, Press & Media Campaigns and Engaging Parliament towards enactment of ALSA.

Maria Chikara Foundation (MCF)

• The Maria Chikara Foundation, is a Zimbabwean registered organization dedicated to empowering the Youth, and catering to the local maternal, pre-natal and infant needs of our communities. As well as podiatric and geriatric care of our elderly.
• We are working closely with the Government and other local NGO’s in the SAY NO TO DRUGS Programme as well as other community development initiatives.
• As the MCF we are currently in the final stages of construction at our first Oasis Clinic & Rehabilitation Facility in Kadoma, and shoring in Zimbabwean Healthcare professionals in the UK currently working for the NHS to participate and bring First World care into our local initiatives.
