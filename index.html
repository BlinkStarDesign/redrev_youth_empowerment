About Us


The RedRev Youth Empowerment Corporation was formed in 2018 by Children of War Veterans in Zimbabwe with the support of the Government. Our goal is to play a central role as the Youth in developing our great Nation into a middle-income Economy by the year 2030. Our investments vehicle currently has active commercial projects in the Sustainability (Green Economy), Mining and beneficiation, Agri-Business, ICT, Energy and Tourism sectors. We now have an Offshore Office in Chelmsford, UK, and active engagements are being made to engage with International Capital Markets to bring back investment in line with the Zimbabwe Open for Business Policy.

Agri-Business

Under this Business Unit, we focus on the Production, Processing, Packaging, and Distribution of agricultural produce to local, regional and international markets.


AGRI-HUBS

RedRev Youth Empowerment Corp is currently working with the Ministry of Agriculture under MOU towards the facilitation of the AGRI-HUBS Programme.


- Hampton Farm HUB currently undergoing an infrastructure overhaul.
- US$ 80K earmarked for production & infrastructure development of AGRI-HUB Facilities in 2024.
- 5 tonnes of processed & dehydrated Cabbage and 25 tons of Soyabean has been produced in the 2022-2023 Season.
- Turnover of US$ 100K per Season once infrastructure development is complete.
- This project has a high ESG Score through it's active empowerment of women & the youth within the Hubs. Climate-friendly methods are also used. - Combined hectarage of 1000Ha earmarked for production in the Year 2024.
- Agro-Processing of Cereal, Horticulture, and Livestock produced is done On-Site.

Hampton Farm Produce Project Stats (2022-2023) CABBAGE CROP

- Project capitalized through the Youth EmpowerBank.
- 20 tonnes of Fabiola Cabbage produced and sold during the 2022 2023 Farming Season.
- 5 tons of processed dehydrated cabbage is currently on-site.
- Hampton Farm Produce, a subsidiary of RedRev Youth Empowerment Corp is responsible for marketing and distribution of the processed goods to Markets.
- Supply contract with Pick'n Pay into Namibia, Botswana, and DRC markets clinched. Local sales at US$1 per Unit.

Hampton Farm Produce Project Stats (2022-2023) SOYABEAN CROP

- Project capitalized through the Youth EmpowerBank.
- 50 tonnes of Soyabean produced and sold during the 2022-2023 Farming Season.
- Urban Basics Co. in India current offtaker through facilitation by AMA.
- Hampton Farm Produce, a subsidiary of RedRev Youth Empowerment Corp is responsible for marketing and distribution of the processed goods to Markets.
- Producer prices currently at US$800 per Tonne.
